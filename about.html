About NEVCO ELECTRIC

NEVCO ELECTRIC SUPPLY INC is a leading wholesale distributor of electrical equipment and supplies in Hallandale Beach, FL. With years of industry experience, we provide comprehensive electrical solutions to contractors, industrial facilities, and businesses throughout South Florida. Our commitment to quality products, competitive pricing, and exceptional customer service has established us as a trusted partner in the electrical supply industry.

Our Vision

  • To be the premier wholesale electrical supplier in South Florida, recognized for excellence in product quality and customer service.
  • To lead the industry in providing innovative electrical solutions and sustainable products.
  • To build lasting partnerships with contractors and businesses through reliability and expertise.

Our Mission

  • Provide high-quality electrical products at competitive wholesale prices to meet our customers' diverse needs.
  • Deliver exceptional customer service through expert technical support and efficient order fulfillment.
  • Maintain strong relationships with manufacturers to ensure access to the latest electrical products and technologies.
